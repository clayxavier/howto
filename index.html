How To Make A Clay Xavier Beat


Clay Xavier is a DJ, Music Producer, and Graphic Designer based out of Oakland, CA. His first ever beat tape, "September", was released this year as an experiment to find his sound. This website was created to let you break down a song he created using the sounds from the song. Enjoy!






Play with these sounds! You can activate the sounds by pressing the corresponding number on the keyboard!
